There are a few important things to note about Nick. He is very reliable, knows his stuff, and probably works out every day to lift that backpack full of goodies. N.P. Childe & Co has access to almost anything, asks very few questions, and charges accordingly. Whether you’re running an honest shop or something more creative, the Merchant is always happy to facilitate. Professionally, of course. He is also a great father figure! To you, to your frog, to your mom? To your neighbour!? The main thing is he will gladly help with anything and everything.
