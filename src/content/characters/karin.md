Young, beautiful, proper. A bit naive? Karin and her guard force are here to save the day! The King’s day that is. Growing up within the castle walls meant being part of the guard was her destiny since childhood, having a mother as the captain does that to a girl. Now Karin is the vice-captain herself and she is ready to show the world that being second in command doesn’t mean second in skill! Sometimes it means second in social skills though, but she’s good looking so we’ll let ‘em off the hook.
