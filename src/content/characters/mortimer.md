A rat in his mid-30s (or the equivalent of a rat-life), lived his entire life working for different criminal organizations in the city. What many would consider illegal and immoral, Mortimer sees as an average Tuesday. It's a difficult life, but after recently taking over as the head for the biggest gang in the city Mortimer is desperate to step up. Doing good by his crew is everything to him, suddenly having so many peoples lives depending on him for survival is tough, but he is ready to do whatever it takes.
As a new potion-making establishment has opened, Mortimer decides to try his luck with the new potion maker, since he has been banned from his usual establishments.
